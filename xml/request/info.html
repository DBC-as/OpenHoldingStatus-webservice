Parameters:
  • holdingsRequest
    • authentication:
      • login triple in subtags: groupIdAut, passwordAut, userIdAut
    • responderId:
      • holding-library code, like DK-790900
    • bibliographicRecordId:
      • Record id, ie. faust, isbn, ...
    • bibliographicRecordAgencyId:
      • Record owner (currently not used)
    • pid:
      • Identifier of Open Search-object
    • outputType:
      • json, php, xml (default soap)
    • callback:
      • for json-callback
  • localisationRequest
    • authentication:
      • login triple in subtags: groupIdAut, passwordAut, userIdAut
    • agencyId:
      • Identifier of an agency
    • pid:
      • Identifier of Open Search-object
    • mergePid:
      • Merge lists of agencies across pids
    • outputType:
      • json, php, xml (default soap)
    • callback:
      • for json-callback
